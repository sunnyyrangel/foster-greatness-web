# Foster Greatness

> Lived experience-led community providing lifelong belonging, resources, and support for current and former foster youth nationwide.

**Mission:** Co-create a space where empowerment, healing, and community are front and center for people impacted by the foster system.

**Who We Serve:** Current and former foster youth ages 18+ (no upper age limit) - support that lasts a lifetime.

**Core Values:** Lived Experience-Led | Lifelong Belonging | Dignity & Empowerment

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-12

---

## Find Local Services & Resources

### Local Services Search
https://fostergreatness.co/services
Search thousands of free and low-cost social service programs by ZIP code. Covers 8 categories: food, housing, healthcare, employment, education, transportation, legal services, and family & childcare. Includes community-recommended programs, curated guides and fact sheets, a resource board to save programs, and map view. Powered by Findhelp with Resource Navigator support available.

### Resource Hub
https://fostergreatness.co/resources
Access free personalized support connecting current and former foster youth to housing assistance, employment programs through Staffmark Group, food security, benefits screening through Single Stop, the Foster Greatness Crisis Fund, mental health resources, and comprehensive FAQ.

### Aging Out Guide
https://fostergreatness.co/aging-out
Understand what happens when aging out of foster care at 18, navigate available extended foster care programs by state, access federal programs like ETV, Medicaid extension, and HUD Family Unification Program, and find comprehensive transition support for foster youth entering adulthood.

### Suggest a Resource
https://fostergreatness.co/suggest-resource
Nonprofits, community members, and those with lived experience can recommend local programs and services to be included in our resource directory. Submissions are reviewed by our team to ensure quality and relevance.

## Join Our Community

### About Foster Greatness
https://fostergreatness.co/about
Learn about our lived experience-led organization serving 2,147+ current and former foster youth nationwide through panel discussions, learning workshops, community events, and personalized resource support. Led by Director of Community Affairs Isabel Stasa, MPA.

### Join the Community
https://community.fostergreatness.co
Connect with current and former foster youth ages 18+ in a lifelong community providing peer support, monthly events, Resource Navigator assistance, and authentic belonging—no age cap, free to join. No proof of foster care status required.

### Community Events
https://fostergreatness.co/events
Attend monthly panel discussions with lived experience leaders, learning workshops on financial literacy and storytelling, and community gatherings including cooking events and support spaces. 62 events hosted in 2025.

## Hear Our Stories

### Thriver Stories
https://fostergreatness.co/thriver-stories
Watch and listen to 25 authentic journeys from foster care alumni who have thrived, sharing real experiences of resilience, growth, and success. Over 70 hours of storytelling content available on YouTube and Spotify.

### Storytellers Collective
https://fostergreatness.co/storytellers-collective
Discover our pilot program training foster youth to own their narratives through professional media training, premium storyteller kits, and a national platform. 7 storytellers in the inaugural cohort, including Taylor Rockhold, whose work led Delaware to adopt the Storytelling Companion statewide for every young person entering care.

### Stories Hub
https://fostergreatness.co/stories
Explore comprehensive collection of foster care alumni stories, video testimonials, and lived experience perspectives from our community members nationwide.
