# Foster Greatness

> Lived experience-led community providing lifelong belonging, resources, and support for current and former foster youth nationwide.

**Mission:** Co-create a space where empowerment, healing, and community are front and center for people impacted by the foster system.

**Who We Serve:** Current and former foster youth ages 18+ (no upper age limit) - support that lasts a lifetime.

**Core Values:** Lived Experience-Led | Lifelong Belonging | Dignity & Empowerment

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-18
