# SLIDE 2: The Opportunity

## Content

### Section Header
THE GAP WE FILL

### The Problem (left column or top section)
**Every year, 20,000+ youth "age out" of foster care.**

They leave with no family, no safety net, and a support system that disappears overnight. The result:

- 50% experience homelessness within 4 years
- Only 3-4% earn a college degree
- 70% of girls who age out become pregnant by age 21

The system provides services until 18 or 21—then it's gone.

### Our Solution (right column or middle section)
**Foster Greatness provides what the system doesn't: lifelong community.**

We're a lived experience-led platform where foster youth find:
- Peer connection that lasts a lifetime
- Resources navigated by people who understand
- Community events that build belonging
- Career support that leads to sustainable employment

**Our philosophy: Age in, never age out.**

### What Makes Us Different (bottom section - 3 columns)

| **Built BY Foster Youth** | **Digital-First Community** | **Business-Powered Model** |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Leadership and programs designed by people with lived experience in foster care | 2,000+ members connected 24/7 through our mobile and web platform | Sustainable funding through DGW Branded social enterprise partnership |

## Visual Direction
- **Layout:** Two-column or top/bottom split
- **Left/Top Image:** Use `/images/web images/community-events.jpg` or `/images/community-feature.png`
- **Colors:** White background with navy text, blue accents for stats
- **Stats:** Display the percentages prominently (consider yellow highlight)
- **Three differentiators:** Use icon boxes with navy icons

## Speaker Notes
"Here's the reality: the foster care system is designed to end. At 18 or 21, support disappears—right when young people need it most. Foster Greatness was founded to change that. We're not a program you age out of. We're a community you age into. And we're built by people who've lived this experience, which means we understand what actually works."

# SLIDE 3: What We Do

## Content

### Section Header
PROGRAMS & IMPACT

### Our Platform (featured callout box)
**2,000+ Community Members Nationwide**

A digital platform where foster youth connect 24/7:
- Peer support and mentorship
- Resource sharing
- Event coordination
- Crisis support connections

### Core Programs (2x3 grid or list)

**1. Resource Navigation**
Access to 600,000+ resources with personalized support from Resource Specialists who help navigate housing, food security, scholarships, and benefits.

**2. Employment Pipeline** *(with Staffmark)*
Resume building, mock interviews, career coaching, and direct job placement through our first-of-its-kind comprehensive employment program.

**3. Community Events**
Quarterly gatherings including cooking events, paint nights, open mics, and support groups—building the family many never had.

**4. Learning Workshops**
Expert-led sessions on financial literacy, career development, trauma recovery, and life skills—taught by people with lived experience.

**5. Wish Partnership** *(with One Simple Wish)*
77 wishes granted in 2024—from Nintendo Switches to essential household items—with year-round access for all ages.

**6. Crisis Support**
Emergency assistance and connections when members need it most, including housing emergencies and basic needs.
